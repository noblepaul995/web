Here’s What You’ve Been Waiting for (Spoiler: It’s Not Much)

Hi! I’m Sagnik Bhattacharya, an enthusiastic web developer with expertise in the MERN stack (MongoDB, Express.js, React, and Node.js). I specialize in crafting seamless, efficient, and visually engaging web applications. Since embarking on my web development journey at the start of 2024 , I’ve been driven by a passion for coding, creativity, and problem-solving. From designing intuitive user interfaces with React to implementing robust back-end architectures with Node.js and Express, I thrive on delivering solutions that make a difference. Continuous learning and a commitment to excellence define my approach to development.

When I’m not wrestling with code or making my IDE crash (it’s a talent), you’ll find me buried in books, exploring new worlds, or sketching—because who doesn’t love turning a blank page into a masterpiece or, occasionally, abstract chaos? These hobbies keep me sane, spark my creativity, and remind me there’s life beyond the keyboard… though I often choose to ignore it.

When I’m not coding, reading, or sketching, you can usually find me daydreaming about the perfect algorithm or debating whether coffee or tea fuels creativity better (spoiler: it’s coffee). I believe every bug in my code has a personal vendetta against me, but I get by with a mix of persistence, caffeine, and the occasional Google search. Life’s too short for bad code, so I like to keep things fun—after all, if you can’t laugh at a bug that took hours to fix, are you even really coding?
